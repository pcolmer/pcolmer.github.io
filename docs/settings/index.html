App settings

Settings for the app are accessed differently depending on the device and version of Windows being used:

  • Windows Phone 8.1/Windows 10 Mobile: on the main screen, tap the ... icon and then tap settings
  • Windows 8.1: swipe in from the right-hand side of the screen to access the Settings Charm then tap on Options
Accessing settings on Windows 10

 

When using Windows 10, tap on the hamburger icon in the top-left corner of the app's window, tap on Settings then tap on Options

Name display order

This allows you to change how the names of people are sorted. When you change this option, the information is re-sorted immediately.

Manage OneDrive downloads

This allows you to control whether or not downloads can take place on a network connection that charges for data. Setting this to Prevent avoids the possibility of a high data bill and requires you to use a network connection that doesn’t charge for data, such as Wi-Fi.

Store OneDrive content

This allows you to control whether or not the app stores copies of photos downloaded from OneDrive on the device. Storing copies saves the app from downloading images every time they are required, but it does require additional storage space on the device.

You can control where these copies are stored on your device by setting the first media location on the Manage media page in the app.

Collect telemetry

When enabled, the app sends anonymous usage data. This is used to improve the product.
